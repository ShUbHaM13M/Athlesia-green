The Game Story

This is the story of the Minamoto clan who were the first Samurais to ever wage a war. One of the most influential and powerful warrior families in Mediaeval Japan. Their clan’s colour has always been green which symbolizes the ever growing forest which thrives to give everyone a prosperous life.

Cup of Peace and Prosperity

For hundreds of years this green clan has fought in the tournament of Remon Kiiro (Lemon Yellow) and have defeated the other two contender clans - the fire-powered Red Clan and the Water-fueled Blue Clan. For Years they have played dangerous tournament games and made sure that Green Clan has won the Athlesia cup. A cup that symbolizes - good omen and has always helped restore peace in the land. Whenever the trophy is not in it’s rightful place - which is with the Green Clan the world shakes and crashes and gets disbalanced. Whenever the Green Clan gets the trophy home, peace and prosperity prevails.

Even today, years later the battle continues to win the Athlesia Cup
